About Us

Empowering students through Design and Tech.

Design goes to School is an initiative set to educate senior secondary school students both in private and government schools about the basic design theories, the importance of design, its relevance to daily life and how it influences choices and decision-making from the secondary level of education.
We are not instigating that every student should end up as a designer, this only assists in preparing the youngsters for life in the tertiary institutions and beyond.

Our Approach To DG2S

Our approach is to design solutions that meet the needs of student to become creative thinkers by them the value of observation, sketching, and prototyping.
