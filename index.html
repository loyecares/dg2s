Get Started
We teach design as a problem solving skill.

This is an initiative set to educate secondary school students about the importance of design, it’s relevance to daily life and how it influences choice or decision making from the secondary level of education. We are definitely not instigating that all student should end up as a designer, NO! But this is to prepare them now for life in university and beyond.
